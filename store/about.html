How It Started

The Story Behind UrbanKart

It started in 2021 when Vikram Singh, a software engineer from Gurugram, tried to buy a reliable WiFi security camera for his mother's home in Jaipur. He spent weeks researching — only to receive a cheap, poorly-made camera that stopped working in two months.

Frustrated, he partnered with his wife Meena, a product designer, and they started UrbanKart with a simple promise: only sell products we've personally tested and would put in our own home.

Three years later, we've helped 50,000+ Indian families make their homes smarter — and we're just getting started.

"We don't just sell gadgets. We sell confidence — the confidence of knowing your home is safe, your family is comfortable, and you got a fair deal."

— Vikram Singh, Co-founder & CEO
