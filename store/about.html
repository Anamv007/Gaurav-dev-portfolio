Our Story 🏠

Started in a Gurugram apartment in 2021. Now serving 50,000+ families across India.

Why We Started

Smart Homes Shouldn't Cost a Fortune

In 2021, our founder Gaurav Mehta tried to automate his rented Gurugram flat. He spent weeks researching, only to find gadgets that were either too expensive, didn't support Indian 220V voltage, had zero local support, or arrived from abroad in 3 weeks with no warranty.

"Why should smart home technology be a luxury? India has 300 million homes — families who want their homes to be safer, more comfortable, and energy-efficient. They deserve products that just work."

He reached out to 3 manufacturing partners, tested over 200 products, and rejected 80% of them. The few that made it — genuinely good, certified, Indian-voltage compatible — became the foundation of UrbanKart.

We launched with 3 products and ₹80,000 in initial inventory. Word spread. In 18 months, we've served over 50,000 Indian families across every state.

Our Mission & Values

🧪

Radical Honesty

We reject 60%+ of products we evaluate. If it doesn't work well in Indian conditions, we don't sell it — even if it would make us more money. Our reputation is worth more than any single sale.

🤝

Customer Obsession

We read every review, reply to every WhatsApp message, and use customer feedback to make buying and setting up our products easier. Your satisfaction is not a KPI — it's our reason for existing.

🇮🇳

Built for India, First

Every decision — voltage compatibility, COD availability, Hindi support, delivery reach to tier-3 towns — is made with the Indian consumer front-and-centre. India is not an afterthought for us.
